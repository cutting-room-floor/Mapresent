# Minimum Viable Product

Ideas about what makes for a minimum iteration of the app for internal testing. 

 * 2D map views
 * Timeline markers as points to play back
 * Marker types
   - Zoom
   - Pan
   - Theme switch
   - Show/hide points
   - Show/hide lines
   - Audio
 * Interruptible playback
   - Don't play audio
   - Play continues again
   - Touch highlighting
